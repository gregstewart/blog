Paul Kenney released few days back his equivalent to JUnit for ColdFusion Components: <cfcUnit/>. It requires cfmx 6.1 and the Mach-II framework. The documentation is not complete yet, however the install instructions are up. Be sure to check back!!

Paul has also kindly put up an excerpt from Christian's Cantrell's article “What is Unit Testing?”, which was initially published on the Macromedia DRK 3. The full article was called: "CFUnit Testing Components".
