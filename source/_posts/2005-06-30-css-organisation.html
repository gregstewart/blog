--- layout: post title: CSS organisation tags: [] status: publish type: post published: true meta: {} --- A few week old now, but Doug over at Stop Design has come up with yet another invaluable little trick: when working with large style sheets it can be quite a lot of work to scroll through it in order to locate a certain declaration. His tip, label your sections and prefix the label with "=" like such: [css]/* MISC: =Lists ----------------------------------------------- */[/css] Since "=" is not used in CSS, doing a search for =List will quickly get you to where you need to be.
