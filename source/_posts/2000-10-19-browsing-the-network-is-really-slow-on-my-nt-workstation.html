Browsing the network is really slow on my NT workstation

Ever used Explorer to browse your network and found that it is extremely slow and unresponsive at times? I had a look throught technet to see if there was any information on this problem and found the following command (article Q226370):

RunDll32 advpack.dll,LaunchINFSectionEx C:winntINFmstask.inf,,,256

Apparently it is down to IE4 and it's task scheduler. Applying this command certainly improves the performance of browsing the local area network.
