After a few false starts on Maloriak, we were able to down him again as well and that opened up Atramades for us. Once again not an overly challenging encounter I thought, pretty much tank and spank with a little bit of running round during his air phase. A little tip if you are playing a Paladin, if he targets you with his Roaring Flame Breath, simply use your Hand of Protection and he will shift his attention to someone else during the air phase.
