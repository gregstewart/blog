Another neat little article on running some of your old Windows programs under Linux. The author talks you through choosing the right emulator for your needs, reviews some of the solutions out there and then takes you through a step by step configuration of win4lin as this is the one that suited his needs.

The case is becoming more and more compelling, now I just need to make sure all of my hardware is supported.
