--- layout: post title: searching a tar tags: [] status: publish type: post published: true meta: {} --- No not searching a sailor, but here is a nifty little trick. This command looks into an archive, searches for a string (in this case "cfusion-ear") and spits out the results to screen: [code]gz cat cfmx_61_multiple_instances.tar.gz | tar tf - | grep cfusion-ear[/code]
