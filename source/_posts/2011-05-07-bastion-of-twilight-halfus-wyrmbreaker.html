This is certainly a more entertaining encounter, especially the first time round with the little drakes, Malevolent Strikes and fire combination. Little tip if you are tanking Halfus with the mortal strike abilitty as a Pally, use Hand of Protection followed by Hand of Freedom to reset the Malevolent Strikes debufs and buy yourself a bit of time before having to start your taunt rotation on Halfus.
