--- layout: post title: re-building morecpthosts tags: [] status: publish type: post published: true meta: {} --- A handy way to reload your Qmail control/morercpthosts is to use the "/var/qmail/bin/qmail-newmrh" command. This command re-builds morercpthosts.cdb file without needing to re-cycle Qmail and hence stop your mail delivery service.
