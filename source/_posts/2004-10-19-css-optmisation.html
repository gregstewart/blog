--- layout: post title: CSS optmisation tags: [] status: publish type: post published: true meta: {} --- A couple of articles on optimising your CSS, try writing lean CSS or use short hand techniques. One small thing to be aware off as a comment indicates is to be careful with shorthand as some browsers are known to have a few problems displaying style declarations that use shorthand [the second article gives examples of those techniques and lists browser compatibility]. A good approach to take as a result of this is to start without optmisation techniques and then once you are done, optimise!
