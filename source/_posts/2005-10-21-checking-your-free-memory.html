--- layout: post title: Checking your free memory tags: - ColdFusion status: publish type: post published: true meta: _edit_last: '2' --- Here is a handy little script that I stumbled across a while back (probably on a mailing list), sorry I can't remember who posted it, but for posterity here it is: [code] rt = createObject("Java", "java.lang.Runtime"); freemem=rt.getruntime().freeMemory(); maxmem=rt.getruntime().maxMemory(); usepercent=freemem/maxmem;
Free memory #numberformat(freemem)#
Max memory #numberformat(maxmem)#
Percent use: #numberformat(usepercent * 100)# [/code]
